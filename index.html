A b o u t

We are a specialized security research and development firm providing advanced research capabilities and strategic consultancy services. Our mission is to perform rigorous research, innovative tooling development, and reverse engineering services.

With backgrounds in both defending against threats and exploiting vulnerabilities, we bring a unique perspective shaped by our experience in both offensive and defensive security. This has enabled us to craft practical, effective security strategies rooted in real-world experience across diverse domains.

Inversive Labs is founded by Ignacio Sanmillan, a security researcher with extensive experience in vulnerability research, exploit development, reverse engineering, malware analysis and threat intelligence. His research has led to multiple CVE discoveries in Apple ecosystem components and contributions to major security organizations including CrowdStrike, ESET, and L3 Harris. Ignacio has presented findings at leading security conferences like Virus Bulletin, and his work has been featured in industry publications focusing on advanced persistent threats and state-sponsored cyber operations.
