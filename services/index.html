  • Custom Test Suite Development: Creation of tailored test frameworks specific to protocol architecture and business logic
  • Continuous Testing Infrastructure: Implementation of automated testing pipelines that continuously validate contract security throughout development
  • Actionable Response: Mitigation support for found issues, providing remediation strategies advice and validation

Smart Contract Protection

We implement advanced obfuscation techniques to protect smart contract intellectual property from reverse engineering and unauthorized analysis. Our multi-layered approach create robust protection while maintaining gas efficiency and contract functionality for both EVM and non-EVM ecosystems.

What We Deliver:

  • Anti-Reverse Engineering Protection: Implementation of code transformation techniques that can resist decompilation and analysis tools
  • IP Theft Prevention: Multi-layer protection solutions to safeguard proprietary algorithms, trading strategies, and business logic
  • Cross-Chain Support: Obfuscation solutions optimized for EVM and non-EVM platforms
  • Gas-Optimized Protection: Carefully balanced obfuscation that maintains reasonable gas costs without compromising security
  • Verification & Differential Testing: Specialized validation to ensure obfuscated contracts maintain identical functionality and security properties

  • Legal & Compliance Documentation: Expert technical reports suitable for regulatory filings, legal proceedings, and dispute resolution
  • Post-mortem Analysis: Analysis of Post-mortem transactions to identify root-cause analysis of malicious behavior
