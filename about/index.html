About

Inversive Labs is a boutique R&D security firm specializing in low-level security research and advanced tooling development with a focus in Solana.

We approach systems from first principles, instrumenting bytecode, tracing execution flows, and building custom solutions oriented around fuzzing and security automation to tackle technically complex challenges that require innovative approaches.

Founded by battle-tested security researchers with expertise in offensive security, threat intelligence, and smart contract security.
