About

We approach systems from first principles, instrumenting bytecode, tracing execution flows, and building custom solutions for technically complex challenges that require innovative approaches.

Founded by researchers with expertise in offensive security, threat intelligence, and smart contract security, we bring rigorous analysis and technical excellence.
